The Regular Limerick Comedy Nights

Limerick currently has two Comedy Groups hosting monthly "Closed Mic" gigs.
For these gigs expect 5 or 6 comedians and a MC doing sets on the shorter side.
It'll often be fresher material that's being tried out, but don't worry, they are professionals... at whatever their day job is...

Mic Drop Comedy

First Saturday of every month 8:30pm

The Commercial Bar,
Catherine Street.



Mic Drop Comedy is Limerick's leading regular Stand-Up night.

Every month, they bring in some of the finest Limerick Comedians and many more from around the country.
