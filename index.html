Regular Comedy Nights

Mic Drop Comedy

First Saturday of every month 8:30pm- The Commercial Bar, Catherine Street. Mic Drop Comedy is Limerick's leading regular Stand-Up night. Every month, they bring in some of the finest Limerick Comedians and many more from around the country. Each night would have 5 or 6 comedians doing short sets over the course of the evening.
